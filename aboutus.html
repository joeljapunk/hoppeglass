ABOUT THE COMPANY



Hiphi Glass and Aluminum Services is introduced to the Philippines in the year 2020. It has over 20 years of Professional Experience in China in supplying and installation of Highend Doors and Windows. The company integrates industrial resources from Germany and China to serve the Philippine local market and is one of the most advance companies when it comes to the design and production of an architectural aluminum systems.

To conform with the constant changes, growing demands and trends in this industry, we continuously research and develp our products to meet not only the needs of t he client but also to provide a satisfactory services, and sustainable and innovative solutions.
